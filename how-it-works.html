The Process

From imagination
to your door.

We've designed every step to be simple, transparent and unhurried. Here is exactly what happens from the moment you start your order to the moment it arrives.

01
Start your order

Upload your design, or let us help you create one.

Already have a finished design? Upload it directly — PDF, AI, InDesign or a high-resolution image. Starting from scratch? Our Design Studio walks you through style, colour and wording with a live preview as you go. No commitment until you're ready.

Upload a Design Open the Studio
02
File check

We check your file before it goes anywhere near a press.

The moment we receive your artwork, it passes through a technical review — checking resolution, dimensions, bleed and colour mode. If anything needs attention, we'll tell you clearly and specifically what to fix. Nothing proceeds until the file is ready for print.

03
Digital proof

You approve every detail before we print a single sheet.

Within one working day of your order, you'll receive a digital proof — a screen-accurate representation of exactly how your invitations will print. Review every name, date and line of wording carefully. If anything needs changing, simply let us know and we'll revise it. Nothing goes to press until you say so.

Your sign-off, your responsibility

The digital proof is your final approval. We ask you to read every detail as if you're seeing it for the first time — names, dates, spelling, wording. Once you confirm, we begin printing immediately.

04
Printed with care

Printed on premium papers in the UK.

Your approved design is printed using professional CMYK presses on the paper stock you selected — from smooth 300gsm silk to textured uncoated stocks and translucent vellum. Every order is individually inspected before it is packed. Printing takes 2–3 working days from the moment you approve your proof.

05
Tracked delivery

Dispatched within 5 working days of proof approval.

Your order is packed carefully and sent via tracked courier. You'll receive tracking details the moment it leaves us. We know your invitations need to arrive on time — every order is built around that.

Our Guarantee

If it doesn't match what you approved,
we reprint it. Free.

If your invitations arrive with a print defect, a colour inconsistency, or any production error on our part — anything that doesn't match your approved proof — we will reprint your entire order at no cost. No forms, no argument. We simply put it right.

Print quality

Colour inconsistencies, unclear text, marks or defects not present in your approved proof — we reprint.

Production errors

Wrong size, wrong paper stock, or anything that differs from what was specified in your confirmed order — we reprint.

Transit damage

Arrive damaged in transit? Photograph the packaging and contact us within 14 days. We reprint immediately.

One important thing to know

Your digital proof is your final approval. Our guarantee covers our printing and production — it does not extend to content errors present in your approved proof. That's why we ask you to read it carefully before you confirm. Check every name, date and line of wording.
