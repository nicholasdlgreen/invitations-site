Materials

Our paper stocks

We use a carefully chosen selection of premium papers. Each one is selected for how it feels in the hand as much as how it looks on the eye.

Smooth White
300gsm · Uncoated · Included as standard

Our default paper — crisp, bright white with a smooth matte finish. Prints with exceptional clarity and colour vibrancy. The choice for clean, contemporary designs.

Cotton Rag
400gsm · Textured · +£20 per order

Made from cotton fibres rather than wood pulp, this paper has a distinctive texture and substantial weight. Feels unmistakably luxurious — ideal for classic or heritage-inspired designs.

Vellum Overlay
Translucent · Ethereal · +£35 per order

Semi-transparent and delicate, vellum adds a romantic, otherworldly quality to any design. Most effective layered over a printed card — a stunning finishing touch for botanical or soft floral designs.
