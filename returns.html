01

Your Statutory Rights

Under the Consumer Contracts Regulations 2013, personalised goods that have been made to a customer's individual specification are exempt from the standard 14-day right to cancel. This means that once your proof has been approved and your order has entered production, you do not have an automatic right to cancel, return or receive a refund.

This exemption applies because your order is made uniquely for you and cannot be resold. We strongly encourage you to review your digital proof carefully before approving it — once approved, your order goes straight to print.

02

When We Will & Won't Accept a Claim

✓ We Will Accept a Claim
  • Order arrives damaged in transit
  • Printed item materially differs from approved proof
  • You received the wrong item
  • Order does not arrive within 10 working days of expected delivery
✕ We Cannot Accept a Claim
  • Error was present in your approved proof
  • Poor quality artwork was uploaded and approved
  • You have changed your mind after approval
  • You have changed your mind after production began
  • Colour variation within normal print tolerances

Colour representation on screen may vary slightly from the finished print due to differences in monitor calibration and print processes. Minor colour variation within industry-standard tolerances does not constitute a fault.
